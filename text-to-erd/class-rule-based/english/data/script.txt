The NHL has many teams. Each team is assigned a name, city, coach, captain, and set_of_players. Each player belongs to only one team. The NHL has many players. Each player is assigned a name, position, skill_level, and set_of_injury_records. A team captain is also a player. Each player in the department can be directly captain managed by another player. A game is played between two teams (referred to as host team and guest team) and has a date (such as May 11th, 2017) and a score (such as 4 to 2).
