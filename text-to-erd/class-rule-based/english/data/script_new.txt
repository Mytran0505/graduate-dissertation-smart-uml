An information technology center needs to organize a database to manage practice computer rooms with the following information. Each computer room is identified by a unique room code, room name, and manager's name. Each computer is identified by a unique computer code, CPU speed, RAM capacity, and hard drive capacity. The computer room has many computers. Each subject is identified by a unique subject code, subject name, and course duration. A subject can be practiced on many computers. A computer can be registered for many subjects. When registered, you must know the registration date.
